Digitize Mentorship Program!!!
As part of our initiative for encouraging students to use Computer Science to create projects that impact their community, we have created a mentorship program where we will be pairing up high schools students who are proficient in CS with middle/high school students from underrepresented areas across the nation to teach them various aspects of CS. About Digitize



Digitize Hackathon III is a free 24 hour coding event for high school students in the Bay Area. We specifically focus on our three fundamental themes: Environmental Conservation, Health & Wellness, and Social Welfare, to encourage students to use technology for social good and create projects that impact the community.
